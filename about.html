About Ethical Hacking

Ethical hacking is the process of testing and evaluating the security of computer systems, networks, or web applications to find vulnerabilities before malicious hackers exploit them. This website is designed to provide resources and tutorials to help you learn the basics of ethical hacking and how to protect systems legally and responsibly.

What is Hacking?

Hacking refers to the unauthorized access, disruption, or manipulation of computer systems, networks, or electronic devices. Hacking can be done for various reasons, including:
- Malicious purposes (black hat hacking)
- Ethical purposes (white hat hacking)
- Research and testing

Types of Hacking

- Black Hat Hacking: Malicious hacking with intent to harm or exploit.
- White Hat Hacking: Ethical hacking to improve security and protect systems.
- Grey Hat Hacking: A mix of black and white hat hacking.

Important Concepts

- Network security
